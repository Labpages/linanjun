关于我 / About Me

李南君，博士，毕业于山东大学，目前担任山东云海国创云计算装备产业创新中心有限公司工程师。2024年进入浪潮集团博士后工作站从事博士后研究，研究方向为模式识别及计算机视觉。

Nanjun Li received his Ph.D. from Shandong University and is currently an engineer at Shandong Yunhai Guochuang Cloud Computing Equipment Innovation Center Co., Ltd. In 2024, he joined the Inspur Group Postdoctoral Research Station, focusing on pattern recognition and computer vision.
